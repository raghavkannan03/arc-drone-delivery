# Parts box

Here is a list of parts in the project box and what they do.

Here are battery chargers. The one above it an older battery charger. It doesn’t support 6S batteries like the one on our V2 drone so we bought the one below which does.

The one below a set of black and a red wire sticking out to the right. This is the balance charging cord for our 6S battery.

The positive/negative set of connectors contain the connectors you need to charge the battery. The upper pair closest to the battery in the picture are the XT150 connectors that connect to the 6S battery. Red to red, black to black. The bottommost yellow plug is an XT60 connector that can be used to charge the test drone battery. You should plug in the small drone battery’s balance plug into the charger and set it to do balance charging when charging.

Below is our zed 2i camera and its power/data cord. This will connect to the nvidia jetson.

To the right of the zed there is a Intel Realsense camera. We thought we were going to use this for depth sensing obstacle avoidance sensor, however, once we tried it we realized the depth mapping was only to like 1m and the camera quality was very poor. The zed also has much better sw support.

![20250216_163049.jpg](Parts%20box/20250216_163049.jpg)

Below are all of the electrical/power parts that usually go in the tarrot box.

Top left we have 2 bags of red and black 10 gauge wire. This was used to wire all the ESCs to our power distribution board. They are guage 10 to support the current.

On the left we have a tarot power distribution board that has lots of plugs for both power and data. This did not have xt60 connectors as i thought it did (it has xt90) so it could not be used for our v1 drone and we ordered a different power distribution board instead. We’re just saving it for maybe use with a future drone.

To the right of it, there are some jumper cables. To the right of those there are XT60 connectors for power. To the right there are deans connectors. I used those for connecting the gimbal board with the battery. to the right of those are xt150 connectors for the battery-powerdist board.

to the left of the battery box there are some wires with dupon connectors. i was using those to connect the escs to the flight controller but didn’t need them. below them are a set of gold bannana connectors. these fit into the escs. there’s also a mini power dist board. don’t use this. it’s too small.

finally at the bottom there are 2 escs. the esc with the xt60 missing has a short circuit DO NOT USE IT. maybe repair it. the other i think does not behave properly. On the bottom right there are some spare allens and cords etc.
