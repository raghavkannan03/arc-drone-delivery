## Flying

The only things likely susceptible to failure are the ESCs and motors. If they fail, the drone will fly out of control to one side as the flight controller currently has no way of knowing that they have failed. THIS WILL HAPPEN VERY FAST. Simply be ready to flip the kill or arm switch to let the drone fall to the ground. This is why it’s important with this drone to fly in an open area with nothing below it.
